East Pune vs South Pune: The 2026 Real Estate Showdown

For a Pune investor in 2026, the choice usually boils down to two giants: the East Corridor (Hadapsar-Manjari-Kharadi) and the South Corridor (Undri-Pisoli-NIBM). Both are booming, but for NA Bungalow Plots, the winner is clear based on the latest economic data.

2. ROI & Appreciation 2024-2026

The East Pune Corridor has outperformed the South by nearly 4% in CAGR appreciation for land. This is largely due to the "Cluster Development Model" adopted by developers like Kumar Properties, which creates a premium aura that standalone plots in the South lack.

The Final Verdict

While South Pune offers scenic hills and cooler climates, East Pune (Manjari) is the "wealth-creation" engine. Proximity to the IT heart of the city makes land here a much more liquid asset with higher rental potential for bungalows.
