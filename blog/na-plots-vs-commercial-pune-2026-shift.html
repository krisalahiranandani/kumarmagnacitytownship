NA Plots vs Commercial: The 2026 Investment Shift

For decades, commercial shops were the go-to for Pune's elite investors. But as we move into 2026, a massive shift is occurring. NA (Non-Agricultural) Bungalow Plots are now outperforming commercial assets in both appreciation and stability.

The Rise of the "Safe Asset"

Post-pandemic urban planning and the rise of remote-first IT jobs have changed the landscape. Commercial properties in busy corridors like Hadapsar are facing "Oversupply Fatigue." In contrast, land supply is finite.

Why Investors are Choosing Plots over Shops

  • Lower Entry Point: A premium NA plot at Kumar Magnacity often costs less than a small commercial unit, yet offers 2x the land share.
  • Zero Maintenance: Commercial units come with steep property taxes and maintenance costs. Land appreciates silently with near-zero overhead.
  • Usage Flexibility: You can build a villa, holiday home, or simply hold the land as a generational asset.

ROI Snapshot 2026

Recent market data indicates that while commercial rentals have stagnated at 4-5%, plotted developments in Manjari have seen a 22% capital appreciation in the last 18 months alone.
