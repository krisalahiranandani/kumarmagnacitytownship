Why Solapur Highway Is Pune's Hottest Growth Corridor

The Pune-Solapur Highway (NH-65) corridor from Hadapsar to Manjari has transformed from a quiet suburban stretch to Pune's most valuable real estate belt. Three mega-catalysts are driving this:

Outer Ring Road

The Pune Outer Ring Road intersection at Manjari will create a multi-modal transport hub, multiplying land values.

Metro Phase 2

The extended metro corridor along Solapur Highway will bring station-proximity premiums to Manjari land.

IT Corridor Expansion

Magarpatta and SP Infocity expansion is pushing demand for residential land southward along the highway.
