Essential Legal Documents to Verify

The NA Plot Verification Checklist

PMRDA Sanction Letter: Ensure the layout is approved by the Pune Metropolitan Region Development Authority.
Individual 7/12 Extract: Each plot should have its own extract, not a combined one for the whole township.
RERA Registration: Verify the MahaRERA number to ensure financial discipline.
NA Order: Non-Agricultural conversion order as per government norms.
Demarcation Certificate: Precise boundary marking of your plot.

Why individual "Satbara" (7/12) is Non-Negotiable

In many local plotting projects, buyers are given a "share" of a large land parcel. At Kumar Magnacity, we provide fully sanctioned NA bungalow plots with individual 7/12 extracts.
