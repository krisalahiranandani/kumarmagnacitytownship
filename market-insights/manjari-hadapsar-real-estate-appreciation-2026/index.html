Manjari-Hadapsar 2026: Why NA Bungalow Plots Outperform Apartments

A deep dive into the fundamental unit of wealth creation in Pune East real estate.

The Hadapsar real estate market is undergoing a tectonic shift. For over a decade, investors flocked to high-rise apartments in Magarpatta and Kharadi. However, as we approach 2026, the Manjari-Hadapsar location is seeing a massive surge in demand for NA Bungalow Plots.

The Supply Crises in Pune East

While apartment supply is abundant, branded land is nearly extinct. This scarcity is the primary driver for appreciation. In the 150-acre integrated township of Kumar Magnacity, we are observing a unique trend where land values are appreciating at approximately 15% CAGR, significantly outstripping the 4-6% growth seen in multi-storey residential projects nearby.

Why Land Owners are Winning

Investing in NA Bungalow Plots at Manjari offers three distinct advantages:

  • FSI Advantage: Unlike a flat, you own the land vertically and horizontally. You can build up to 3 floors as per PMRDA norms.
  • Zero Depreciation: Buildings depreciate, but the land beneath them only grows in value.
  • Strategic Location: Being near the EON IT Park and World Trade Center, these plots serve the high-ranking IT leadership who prefer independent luxury living over crowded societies.

For those looking to dominate the East real estate market with a high-ROI asset, the choice is clear. Branded plots like those at Kumar Magnacity offer the safety of a corporate developer with the high-yield potential of raw land.
