Infrastructure Impact: Pune Ring Road & Metro Phase 2 on Manjari Land Prices

Why the Shewalewadi-Manjari corridor is becoming the logistical beating heart of East Pune.

Real estate appreciation is 90% driven by infrastructure. In the Manjari Hadapsar area, the convergence of three mega-projects is creating a "Golden Triangle" of connectivity. For investors at Kumar Magnacity, this translates to high liquidity and rapid capital growth.

1. The PMRDA Pune Ring Road (128 km)

The upcoming Pune Ring Road is the biggest game-changer for land prices. With an exit planned near Shewalewadi, residents of Kumar Magnacity will have direct, signal-free access to Pimpri-Chinchwad, Hinjewadi, and the Mumbai-Pune Expressway. This effectively cuts travel time by 40%, making NA plots in Manjari highly desirable for IT professionals working in the Western corridor.

2. Pune Metro Phase 2 Extension

The proposed Metro Phase 2 extension will connect Hadapsar and Loni Kalbhor, passing through the Manjari micro-market. Metro connectivity historically boosts land prices by 20-25% within a 2km radius of its stations. As a 150-acre integrated township, Kumar Magnacity stands to benefit immensely from this mass transit link to the city center.

3. Solapur Highway & Logistics Hubs

The widening of the Pune-Solapur Highway and the development of logistical hubs in Loni Kalbhor are creating thousands of new high-paying jobs in the vicinity. This demand for premium living in a gated community bungalow plot project is outstripping supply, leading to the current price rally.
