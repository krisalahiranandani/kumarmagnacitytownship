Why NA Plots Are Officially Extinct in Pune

The data doesn't lie — Pune's branded NA plot supply has reached near-zero levels.

The Supply Crisis: 3 Forces That Killed NA Land

PMRDA Zoning Lock

PMRDA's 2024 Master Plan reclassified over 60% of peripheral agricultural land as "green zone" or "public utility," permanently removing it from NA conversion eligibility.

Metro Corridor Acquisition

Pune Metro Phase 2 and 3 corridors have acquired massive land parcels along Hadapsar-Manjari, leaving zero large contiguous plots for new township development.

Apartment Saturation

Developers prefer apartments for higher margins. In 2025, only 3 branded NA plot projects launched in all of Pune — down from 28 in 2019.
